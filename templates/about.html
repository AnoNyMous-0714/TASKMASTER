About PSBA


Founded in October 1963 by dedicated educators and professionals, the Philippine School of Business Administration (PSBA) focuses on developing skilled young men and women to meet the economic needs of the nation. Originally launched as the Philippine Accounting and Taxation Training Services, Inc. (PATTS), a CPA review center, it quickly became one of the largest in the country. In 1966, it was renamed PSBA and began offering business administration programs. PSBA-Manila and its sister school, PSBA-Quezon City, established in 1981, emphasize academic quality and scholarship. PSBA introduced an MBA program in 1979 and a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) program in 1993, catering to advanced management education needs. These programs are primarily on-campus but also offer off-campus classes for business and government executives. PSBA collaborates with educational institutions in Asia, providing technical assistance and academic program design. It has established partnerships with institutions in Indonesia and Pakistan, offering advisory services and qualified instructors.


About the Software

Our mission is to streamline workflows and enhance productivity through innovative task management solutions. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, we aim to empower students, faculty, and staff with efficient tools that simplify task organization, collaboration, and achievement.
